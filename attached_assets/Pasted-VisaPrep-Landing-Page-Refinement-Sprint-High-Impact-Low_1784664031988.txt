VisaPrep Landing Page Refinement Sprint (High Impact, Low Effort)

The AI engine and product experience have matured significantly. The landing page should now better reflect the quality of the product behind it.

This is not a redesign. It is a high-impact visual refinement intended to make the product feel more premium and more confident, especially on desktop, while preserving the existing branding, colors, copy, and functionality.

Please implement the following changes:

1. Increase the desktop content width (Highest Priority)

The current layout feels too narrow on desktop and resembles a mobile application centered on a large screen.

Increase the maximum content width so the page makes much better use of desktop space while maintaining comfortable readability.

Do not simply stretch text across the entire screen. Create a balanced, premium layout with generous margins.

2. Increase hero typography

Make the hero section feel more confident and visually important.

Increase the size and weight of:

VisaPrep logo/wordmark (slightly)
Hero headline
Supporting paragraph
Green emphasis statement

The typography should immediately communicate that this is a serious AI platform rather than an early-stage demo.

Maintain excellent readability on mobile.

3. Add more whitespace

Increase vertical spacing between major sections:

Logo
Hero headline
Supporting paragraph
Green emphasis statement
Upload card

Avoid making the page feel crowded.

Aim for a calm, premium presentation similar to modern AI products.

4. Make the upload card more prominent

Slightly increase the visual presence of the upload card.

Without changing its functionality:

give it a little more breathing room
slightly increase its visual weight
ensure it naturally draws the user's attention as the primary action on the page

Do not make it oversized.

5. Improve desktop visual hierarchy

Review the desktop experience as a whole.

Ensure that users immediately understand:

What VisaPrep is
Why it is valuable
What they should do next

The eye should naturally flow:

VisaPrep

↓

Your interview starts with your application.

So do we.

↓

Supporting paragraph

↓

Understand your application.

Prepare with clarity.

Interview with confidence.

↓

Upload your DS-160

all existing colors

The objective is simply to make the existing landing page look more premium and more confident.

Think of this as moving from an MVP appearance toward the visual polish expected of a professional AI SaaS product.

Someone visiting the page on a desktop should immediately feel:

"This looks like a serious AI platform."
