Prompt 2 — Insight Quality Polish (Run after Prompt 1)

VisaPrep Insight Quality Polish



The architecture is complete.



Do not change:



schema;

UI;

prompt philosophy;

AI Constitution;

session version;

temperature;

free/paid boundary.



This sprint only improves the quality of selected insights.



1. Strengthen Relationship Interpretation



When a relationship exists, prefer naming the specific connected facts.



Avoid:



Your circumstances have improved.



Prefer:



Your promotion, increased income, stronger savings, and additional international travel together present a materially different profile.



Avoid:



Your role relates to the conference.



Prefer:



Because the conference focuses on offshore technologies relevant to your employer's operations, your attendance aligns naturally with your current responsibilities.



Specificity should always be preferred over abstraction.



2. Improve Educational Relationships



When analyzing F-1 applications:



Prefer relationship-based educational insights over descriptive educational summaries.



Highest priority:



Computer Science



↓



Data Analytics



↓



Career Goal



↓



Contribution in Nigeria



Avoid generic wording like:



Your chosen program aligns with your goals.



Instead explain:



why the previous education,



the new degree,



and the future career



reinforce one another.



3. Improve Highlight Selection Quality



Maintain exactly three Application Highlights for the free Application Insights report.



Do not increase or decrease the number of highlights.



Instead, improve the quality of the three selected highlights.



When multiple valid highlights exist, always prefer the three that provide the greatest interpretive value to the applicant.



Relationship-based insights should generally outrank descriptive or administrative observations.



The objective is not to show more highlights, but to ensure the three shown are the most meaningful.
