━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Our goal is for VisaPrep to feel like an experienced visa coach helping the applicant understand their own application.

Review the Stage 2 prompt and improve it according to these principles.

1. Teach instead of merely state.

Whenever "Why this may come up" simply repeats the observation, rewrite it so it genuinely explains WHY the topic may naturally arise during the interview.

Good example:

Observation:
"Your application indicates that you plan to attend a professional forum in Houston."

Weak:
"Officers often ask about the purpose of travel."

Better:
"A consular officer may explore this to better understand how attending the forum relates to your professional responsibilities and how it fits within your overall travel plans."

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2. Remove unnecessarily intimidating language.

Avoid wording like:

- assess your intentions
- determine whether you qualify
- evaluate your eligibility
- establish credibility
- verify your ties

Replace these with educational language such as:

- better understand...
- clarify...
- understand the relationship between...
- understand how...
- discuss...

The applicant should feel informed, not judged.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3. Reduce repetition.

Avoid repeating nearly identical information across:

Observation

↓

Why this may come up

↓

What you should be ready to explain

Each section should contribute new understanding.

Observation = What the application says.

Why this may come up = Why that detail may naturally become part of the interview.

What you should be ready to explain = What the applicant should prepare to discuss.

Each section has a different purpose.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4. Make explanations more applicant-centered.

Prefer:

"Your application indicates..."

"A consular officer may want to better understand..."

instead of

"The applicant..."

"Officers assess..."

The conversation should feel personal.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

5. Strengthen educational value.

Every explanation should answer:

"So what?"

The applicant should finish reading every preparation area with a better understanding of why that topic matters.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6. Preserve ethics.

Do not encourage memorization.

Do not generate scripts.

Do not predict interview outcomes.

Continue emphasizing honest preparation and clear explanation.
